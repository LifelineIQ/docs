Use **Display Devices** to review device status and assign devices to displays.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Display Devices">
    Go to **Display > Devices**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the device">
    Search devices or filter by status, such as **Needs display**, **Assigned / active**, **Online**, **Offline**, or **Error**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open device actions">
    Use the device action menu or select the device row.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign the device">
    Open the assign dialog and choose the display the device should show.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh or reboot if needed">
    Use device actions to refresh or reboot when the device is stuck or stale.
  </Step>
</Steps>
