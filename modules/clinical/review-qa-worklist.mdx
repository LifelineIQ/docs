Use the QA worklist to find clinical quality reviews that need attention.

## Before you start

Open **Clinical QA**. The current route is `/clinical/qa`.

You need quality access to use QA workflows.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Clinical QA">
    Go to **Clinical QA**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review assigned work">
    Look for incidents assigned to you or work that needs QA review.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open an incident">
    Select the incident that needs review.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start or continue the QA review">
    Open the QA review workspace for that incident.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ shows

The QA worklist focuses reviewers on incidents that need quality review. The exact list depends on your access and QA configuration.

## Common problems

### I cannot access the QA worklist

You may not have quality access. Ask an administrator to review your role and clinical quality permissions.

### I do not see an expected incident

Check whether it belongs in **All QA incidents**, whether it has been assigned, or whether your filters are hiding it.
