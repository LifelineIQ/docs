Clinical Quality is where authorized users review incidents, QA assignments, and quality follow-up.

The **CareIQ** console at **Quality → CareIQ** brings clinical QA reviews, pending patient survey responses, and patient complaints and compliments into a single worklist. Reviewers can complete each item in place. It complements the traditional QA worklist below.
