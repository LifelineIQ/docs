Complete a QA review when an incident needs clinical quality review and follow-up.

## Before you start

Open the incident from the QA worklist or the all-incidents QA view. QA review routes include `/clinical/qa-review/incident/:incidentId`.

You need quality access to complete the review.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the QA review">
    Open the incident from the QA worklist.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the incident">
    Check the clinical information available to your role.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Record findings">
    Complete the QA review fields required by your agency's quality process.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add follow-up if needed">
    Document any required education, remediation, or follow-up workflow.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the review">
    Save the review when the QA work is complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ records

LifelineIQ keeps QA review work connected to the incident so quality history, follow-up, and reporting stay together.

<Warning>
  Do not copy patient information into documentation, screenshots, or support examples.
</Warning>

## Common problems

### I cannot complete the review

Check whether required QA fields are missing or whether your role has permission to complete the review.

### The incident is missing information

Follow your agency's process for requesting clarification or additional documentation before completing the QA review.
