Use **Chart** to document a patient care report.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Chart">
    Go to **Chart**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a chart">
    Select **New chart**. LifelineIQ starts a new chart and opens the chart editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link or confirm the patient">
    Add or confirm the patient when the chart requires patient information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete chart sections">
    Work through the sections that apply, including patient, history, situation, assessment, vitals, medications, procedures, times, scene, response, disposition, destination, handoff, mileage, narrative, forms, billing, signatures, crew, corrections, outcome, and registries.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use quick actions when needed">
    Use quick vitals, medication, procedure, or note tools when they help document faster.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review validation">
    Run or review NEMSIS validation and fix required findings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark complete">
    Select the mark-complete action when the chart is ready and validation allows completion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### I cannot complete the chart

Review validation findings and required sections. The complete action is only safe when required chart checks pass.

### The chart is read-only

Completed, locked, or finalized charts may not allow normal editing. Use the corrections workflow if your role can correct it.
