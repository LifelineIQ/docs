---

Use print view when you need a printable or exportable chart copy.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the chart">
    Go to **Chart** and open the patient care report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open print view">
    Open the chart's print route or print action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the print view">
    Confirm the chart content, corrections, and signatures look right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Print">
    Select **Print**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export if needed">
    Use the export action when you need a PDF file instead of a printed copy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### The chart content looks incomplete

Return to the chart editor and confirm the chart sections were saved and completed.

### I cannot export

Check your role and whether the chart status allows export.
