## Where to go

- Open **Chart** for the chart workbench.
- Open **New Chart** to start a new patient care report.
- Use chart views such as **My open** and **My completed** to find work.
- Open **Admin > Chart** for lists, custom forms, section rules, validation, protocols, features, and AI settings.
