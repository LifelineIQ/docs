## Where to go

- Open **Intelligence** for the Intelligence hub.
- Open **Reports** for module reports.
- Open **Dashboards** for dashboards and dashboard editing.
- Open **Report Inbox** for scheduled report deliveries.
- Open **Audit** for audit views.
- Open **Forecasting** for forecasting and scenario planning.
- Open **Data** for the Data Hub.
- Open **Automation** for workflows, templates, history, policies, and AI agents.
- Open **Admin > Intelligence** or **Admin > Data** for setup.
