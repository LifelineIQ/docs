Use **Review Queue** for reports pending QA review.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Review Queue">
    Go to **Fire Reporting > Review Queue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the report">
    Review the report number, QA status, created date, and available actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start or open review">
    Select **Start Review** or **Open Review**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete QA review">
    Review the report details and record QA findings or follow-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve or return">
    Select **Approve** when the report is ready, or return it when the crew needs to correct it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### The report is not in the queue

Only reports in review status appear in the Review Queue. Check **All Reports** or **Drafts**.

### The report needs correction

Return the report instead of approving it. The crew can reopen the report and correct the needed fields.
