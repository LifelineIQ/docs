Fire Reporting is where fire teams create incident reports, manage QA review, submit to NERIS, and maintain related prevention and readiness records.

## Where to go

- Open **Fire Reporting** for the command center.
- Open **New Report** to create a report.
- Open **All Reports** or **Drafts** to find reports.
- Open **Review Queue** for QA review.
- Open **NERIS Submissions** and **Rejected** for submission follow-up.
- Open **Properties & Preplans**, **Inspections & Code**, **Permits**, or **Hydrants** for prevention work.
- Open **Admin > Fire Reporting** for NERIS, apparatus, templates, and submission settings.
