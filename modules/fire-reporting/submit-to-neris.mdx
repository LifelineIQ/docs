Use **NERIS Submissions** to track fire report submission status.

## Submit or resubmit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open submissions">
    Go to **Fire Reporting > NERIS Submissions**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the report">
    Review the report status, submission status, submitted time, NERIS UID, and any last submission error.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit or resubmit">
    Select the submit or resubmit action when the report is ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch the status">
    A successful send means NERIS received the report. LifelineIQ may show **Awaiting NERIS** until NERIS accepts or rejects it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Correct a rejected submission

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open rejected submissions">
    Go to **Fire Reporting > Rejected**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the error">
    Read the last submission error and identify what must be corrected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reopen the report">
    Use the action to reopen the report for correction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Correct and resubmit">
    Correct the report, return it through review if required, and resubmit to NERIS.
  </Step>
</Steps>
