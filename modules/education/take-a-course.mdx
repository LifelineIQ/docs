Use **My Learning** to see courses assigned to you and continue training.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open My Learning">
    Go to **Education > My Learning**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the course">
    Review **Overdue**, **Due now**, **Upcoming & assigned**, and **Completed** sections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the course">
    Select the course you need to take.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete lessons">
    Work through the course lessons. When a lesson is finished, select **Mark complete**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the assessment">
    If the course includes an assessment, select **Start**. If you need another attempt later, use **Retake** when it is available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm completion">
    Return to **My Learning** and confirm the course appears as completed or shows updated progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### I do not see the course

Check that you are in the correct agency and that the course has been assigned to you.

### My lesson still shows incomplete

Open the lesson again and select **Mark complete** after finishing the required content.

### I failed or need to retake an assessment

Use **Retake** if it is available. If it is not available, contact your education lead.
