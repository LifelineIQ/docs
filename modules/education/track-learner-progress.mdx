Use **Progress Tracking** when you need to see where a learner stands across courses, requirements, and signoffs.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Progress">
    Go to **Education > Progress**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a learner">
    Use the **Learner** selector. If no learner is selected, LifelineIQ may show the first active learner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter the transcript">
    Use start and end dates, program, cohort, requirement type, and status filters when you need a narrower view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review completion">
    Check course completion, competency progress, field training progress, and transcript requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open supporting sections">
    Review NCCP tracking, field training packet details, and signoff history when they apply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export if needed">
    Use the export action to send or create an education file for the selected learner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### I do not see a learner

Check the learner roster and make sure the person is active and enrolled in the correct program or cohort.

### The transcript looks incomplete

Clear filters first. Then check whether the course, competency, or field training item has been assigned and completed.
