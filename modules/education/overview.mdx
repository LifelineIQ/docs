## Where to go

- Open **Education** for the education dashboard.
- Open **My Learning** for courses assigned to you.
- Open **Courses** for the course catalog.
- Open **Learners** for roster and cohort enrollment.
- Open **Progress** for transcripts, milestones, and signoffs.
- Open **Field Training** for shift logs and field training work.
- Open **Compliance** for recertification and credential tracking.
- Open **Reports** for education reporting.
- Open **Admin > Education** for programs, courses, learners, reports, and accreditation settings.
