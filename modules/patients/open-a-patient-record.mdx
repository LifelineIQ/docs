Open a patient record when you need to review patient-related information in LifelineIQ.

## Before you start

Find the patient from **Patients** first. The patient record route is `/patients/:id`.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Patients">
    Go to **Patients**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the patient">
    Search or filter until you find the right patient.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the record">
    Select the patient row or record link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the information you need">
    Use the patient record sections available to your role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ records

LifelineIQ keeps patient-related information connected to the patient record. Access to patient information should follow your agency policy and LifelineIQ permissions.

<Warning>
  Do not add patient information to docs, screenshots, support messages, or examples.
</Warning>

## Common problems

### I cannot open the record

You may not have access to that patient or agency. Confirm your active agency and ask an administrator if the access looks wrong.

### The patient information looks incomplete

Check whether related workflows, imports, or records have finished syncing before changing the record.
