Use the patient directory when you need to find a patient record.

## Before you start

Open **Patients**. The current route is `/patients`.

Only search for patient information when you have a work reason to do so.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Patients">
    Go to **Patients**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search or filter">
    Use the available search and filters to narrow the directory.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check the match">
    Confirm you are opening the correct patient before you select the record.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the record">
    Select the patient to open their record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ shows

LifelineIQ opens the patient record page for the selected patient. What you can see depends on your role and permissions.

## Common problems

### I cannot find the patient

Check spelling, try a broader search, or confirm the patient belongs to the agency you are working in.

### I found more than one possible match

Do not guess. Use the information your role allows you to confirm before opening or changing a record.
