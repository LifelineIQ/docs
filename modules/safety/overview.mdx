## Where to go

- Open **Safety** for the Safety hub.
- Open **Checks & Equipment** for daily, station, and unit checks.
- Open **Incidents** for reported safety incidents.
- Open **Report** for a field safety report.
- Open **Fatigue** for fatigue monitoring.
- Open **OSHA Log** for OSHA recordkeeping.
- Open **Admin > Safety** for check templates.
