Use Safety QA or Security QA to review operational chart quality work.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the QA list">
    Go to **Ops Charts > Safety QA** or **Ops Charts > Security QA**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the chart">
    Use the table and status filters to find the chart needing review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the chart">
    Select the chart to open the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review QA details">
    Review chart content, QA status, tasks, approvals, links, and attachments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save updates">
    Save changes or use the available QA/status actions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
