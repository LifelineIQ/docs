The CAD board is the Dispatch front door.

## Before you start

Open **Dispatch**. The current Dispatch root sends you to `/dispatch/schedule`.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dispatch">
    Go to **Dispatch**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the board">
    Use the board to see active operational work, resource context, and dispatch status.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the related workflow">
    Use the board links or Dispatch navigation to open incidents, recurring trips, resources, or other related work.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Keep the board current">
    Update operational information as work changes during the shift.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ changes

Dispatch updates can affect operational views, resource status, shift reports, and downstream incident workflows.

## Common problems

### I landed on the schedule board instead of an old Dispatch page

That is expected. Older Dispatch dashboard and CAD links now resolve to the current Dispatch board.

### I need Dispatch settings

Operational settings live under **Admin → Dispatch**.
