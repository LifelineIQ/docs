Create an incident when a new dispatch event needs to be tracked in LifelineIQ.

## Before you start

Open **Dispatch** and go to **Incidents**. The current route is `/dispatch/incidents`.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dispatch incidents">
    Go to **Dispatch** and open **Incidents**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start a new incident">
    Use the new incident action. Older new-call links route to the incidents page with the new incident flow open.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter required details">
    Add the required dispatch information for the incident.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the incident">
    Save once the required information is complete.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Continue dispatch work">
    Use the incident record and Dispatch board to continue managing the event.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ records

LifelineIQ creates a dispatch incident record and connects later operational updates to that incident.

## Common problems

### The new incident screen did not open

Go directly to **Dispatch → Incidents** and start the new incident action from there.

### Required information is missing

Complete the required fields before saving. If the information is not available yet, follow your agency's dispatch process.
