Use **Onboarding Manager** to review assigned agency onboarding progress.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Onboarding Manager">
    Go to **Onboarding > Manager**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a status filter">
    Filter agencies by active, blocked, or complete status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review blocked tasks">
    Open blocked task groups and read the blocker reason.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the next action">
    Enter the next internal action and select **Save note**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open an agency step">
    Select an active agency card to open its onboarding step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Onboarding manager notes should not include patient, claim, or other PHI details.
</Warning>
