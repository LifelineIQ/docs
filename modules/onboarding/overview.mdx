## Where to go

- Open **Onboarding** for agency onboarding.
- Open **Onboarding Manager** to review assigned agency onboarding progress.
- Open **Launchpad** for first-login launch work.
- Open **Admin > Provider > Onboarding** for onboarding templates and settings.
