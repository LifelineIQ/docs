Use **Onboarding** to complete agency setup before go-live.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Onboarding">
    Go to **Onboarding**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your setup track">
    Select the role track that matches your work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete each step">
    Enter the requested agency, contact, branding, personnel, service, unit, station, payer, fee schedule, banking, licensing, EHR, and authorization information that applies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import documents if needed">
    Use the document import action when setup information can be imported from a file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save progress">
    Select **Save now** when you want to save before moving on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Flag a blocker if needed">
    Use **Flag step as blocked** when you cannot complete a step yet. Add only operational notes, not patient or claim details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Select **Complete onboarding** after required activation milestones are complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### Complete onboarding is disabled

Finish the required critical milestones first. LifelineIQ shows the next milestone that still needs attention.

### A step is blocked

Flag the step and explain the next needed action. Onboarding managers can track it from the manager dashboard.
