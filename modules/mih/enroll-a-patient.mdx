Use **Enrollment** to move accepted referrals into active MIH programs.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Enrollment">
    Go to **MIH > Enrollment**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the patient">
    Search by patient or program, or filter by status such as **Awaiting Enrollment**, **Enrolled**, **Active**, **On Hold**, **Completed**, or **Discharged**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start enrollment">
    Select **Enroll** on the accepted referral row.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete enrollment details">
    Choose the program and complete the required enrollment information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save enrollment">
    Save the enrollment. The patient becomes available in MIH patient and caseload workflows.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### No referrals are ready to enroll

Open **Referral Inbox** and accept the referral first.

### I need to see the original referral

Use **View Referral** from the enrollment row when available.
