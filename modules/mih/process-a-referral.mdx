Use **Referral Inbox** to review new MIH referrals and decide the next step.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Referral Inbox">
    Go to **MIH > Referrals**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the referral">
    Search referrals or filter by status, such as **Pending**, **Accepted**, or **Rejected**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the referral">
    Select the referral row to review details, attachments, readiness badges, and partner loop status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review readiness">
    Confirm consent, assignment, and referral packet readiness before moving the referral forward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept or reject">
    Select **Accept** when the referral is ready for enrollment, or reject it when the referral should not move forward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move to enrollment">
    After acceptance, select **Proceed to Enrollment** or open **MIH > Enrollment**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### I do not see the referral

Check the agency selection, clear filters, and search by patient or referral details.

### The packet is not ready

Review consent, assignment, and attachments. Mark the referral ready only when the intake packet is complete.
