Use the MIH visit chart to document a scheduled or active MIH visit.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the visit">
    Open the visit from **MIH > Schedule**, **Dispatch**, a patient record, or another MIH workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the visit">
    If the visit is scheduled, select **Start Visit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document the visit">
    Complete the visit sections that apply, such as subjective, assessment, phone notes, follow-up, vitals, medications, interventions, education, care plan updates, billing, and signatures.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save section work">
    Use each section's save action as you document. LifelineIQ also shows save status in the chart workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the visit">
    Select **Complete Visit** when documentation is finished.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attest or co-sign">
    If the visit requires attestation, open the attestation dialog and confirm the finalized visit record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### The chart is read-only

Completed or finalized visits may be read-only. Use the available addendum or attestation workflow if corrections are allowed.

### Billing is not ready

Review the billing section and then check **MIH > Billing Queue** for billing readiness follow-up.
