Mobile Integrated Health, or MIH, is where teams manage community paramedicine and mobile health work from referral through enrollment, visits, tasks, billing readiness, and reporting.

## Where to go

- Open **MIH** for the dashboard.
- Open **Referrals** for the referral inbox.
- Open **Import Queue** for imported referrals.
- Open **Enrollment** for accepted referrals and enrolled patients.
- Open **Patient List** or **My Caseload** for patient work.
- Open **Schedule**, **Dispatch**, **Comm Center**, or **Pathways** for clinical operations.
- Open **Tasks** for MIH task work.
- Open **Billing Queue** for billing readiness.
- Open **Reporting**, **SDOH Analytics**, or **Reimbursement** for MIH insights.
- Open **Admin > MIH** for MIH settings.
