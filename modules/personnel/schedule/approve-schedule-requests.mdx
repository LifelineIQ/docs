Use schedule requests to review changes that affect staffing.

## Before you start

Open **Schedule Requests** from the schedule area. The current route is `/schedule/requests`.

You need permission to approve schedule or leave requests.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open requests">
    Go to **Schedule Requests**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Filter the list">
    Filter by request type, status, or station if needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the request">
    Check the person, request type, affected shift or leave dates, reason, and staffing impact.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approve or deny">
    Approve requests that are safe for coverage. Deny requests that cannot be supported, and include a reason when needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ changes

Approved requests update the related schedule or leave record. Denied requests keep the schedule unchanged and notify the requester through the normal request workflow.

## Common problems

### A request would leave a coverage gap

Do not approve until the shift is covered or leadership accepts the coverage risk.

### A swap does not look right

Open the related schedule context before approving. Confirm both sides of the swap are correct.
