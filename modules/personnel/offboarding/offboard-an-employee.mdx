Offboard an employee when they leave the organization and their records need to remain available for history, compliance, and audits.

## Before you start

Open **Personnel** and find the staff member. The current route is `/personnel`.

You need permission to update personnel records.

<Warning>
  Do not delete employee history to offboard someone. LifelineIQ keeps records so prior schedules, timecards, credentials, and audit history remain traceable.
</Warning>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Personnel">
    Go to **Personnel**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the staff member">
    Search or filter the list until you find the employee.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the offboarding action">
    Use the staff member actions menu and choose the offboarding or termination action.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the change">
    Confirm that you want to offboard the staff member.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review follow-up work">
    Check any onboarding/offboarding checklist items, access tasks, schedule cleanup, and payroll/timecard review that apply.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ changes

LifelineIQ marks the staff member as inactive or terminated while keeping their historical records. Their prior schedules, timecards, documents, credentials, and audit history remain available where permitted.

## Common problems

### The employee is still showing in active lists

Refresh the list and check the status filter. Some views have filters for active, offboarding, and terminated staff.

### The employee still appears on a schedule

Review future schedules and remove or replace the assignment where needed.

### Payroll still needs final time

Review the employee's timecards before final payroll export.
