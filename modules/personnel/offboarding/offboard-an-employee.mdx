Use **Provider \> People** when an employee leaves the agency or needs to be removed from active staffing workflows. LifelineIQ keeps the employee's history so schedules, timecards, credentials, property, documents, and audits remain traceable.

<Frame caption="Personnel People list with the Offboard action. Employee rows are blurred for privacy.">
  ![Personnel People page showing employee filters, status tabs, Offboard, Invite to platform, and Hire actions.](/images/screenshots/personnel-people.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  Do not delete historical employee records to offboard someone. Use the offboarding or termination workflow so prior activity stays available for reporting and audits.
</Warning>

## Before you start

You need permission to update personnel records for the active agency.

Before terminating someone, confirm your agency's HR process for final shifts, timecards, property, credentials, documents, and system access.

## What this page is for

Use this workflow when you need to remove an employee from active personnel operations while keeping their LifelineIQ record. Depending on where you start, LifelineIQ may show the quick **Offboard Employee** confirmation from the People list or the fuller **Terminate employee** dialog on a profile.

## Steps from the People list

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open People">
    Go to **Provider** and select **People**. The current route is `/personnel`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the employee">
    Use the status tabs, search box, filter button, or table sorting to find the employee.

    Useful tabs include **All**, **Active**, **New hires**, **Probationary**, **Out of Compliance**, **On Leave**, **Offboarding**, and **Terminated**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the employee">
    Check the box beside the employee if you are using the bulk **Offboard** action, or open the employee row/profile if you need the full profile workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Offboard">
    Select **Offboard** from the top action bar or the employee's row actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Offboard Employee">
    In the **Offboard Employee** confirmation, read the message carefully. It explains that LifelineIQ marks the employee as terminated but keeps their records for historical purposes.

    Select **Offboard** only when you are sure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Steps from the employee profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the employee profile">
    From **People**, select the employee's row to open their profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Terminate employee">
    Open the employment action in the profile header and select **Terminate employee**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter termination details">
    In the **Terminate employee** dialog, choose **Termination type**, enter **Last day of work**, and enter the **Reason for termination**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload or skip the notice if required">
    If your agency settings require a termination notice, upload the termination letter or notice document. If your policy allows it, select **Skip**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm">
    Review the type, last day, reason, and notice status. Select **Terminate employee** to finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Follow-up checklist

After offboarding or termination, review these items before considering the process complete:

- Remove or replace future schedule assignments.
- Review final timecards and pending time entries.
- Collect or mark returned property and equipment.
- Review credentials, compliance items, and document retention needs.
- Remove platform access if your agency process does not do it automatically.
- Check payroll, benefits, and HR tasks outside LifelineIQ if they apply.

## What LifelineIQ changes

LifelineIQ updates the employee's employment status to inactive or terminated, keeps historical records, and can create or show offboarding checklist work when that workflow is configured.

## Common problems

### The employee still appears in active lists

Refresh the People page and check the active status tab. Some views include inactive or terminated employees when filters are set to **All**.

### The employee still appears on future shifts

Open **Schedule** and remove or replace future assignments. Terminating a profile does not automatically ensure every future schedule slot is correctly covered.

### I terminated the wrong employee

Stop and notify an administrator immediately. Use the employee profile history and offboarding checklist to determine what changed before making further edits.

### I cannot see Terminate employee

You may not have permission, or you may be in a list view that only supports the quicker **Offboard** action. Open the employee profile or ask an administrator to complete the action.
