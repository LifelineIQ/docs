Clock out from the same Relay clock bar you used to start your shift.

## Before you start

You must have an open clock-in. If the clock bar shows **Off Duty**, LifelineIQ does not see an active shift for you.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Relay">
    Go to **Relay**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your status">
    Confirm the clock bar shows **On Duty**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clock out">
    Select **Clock Out**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the shift ended">
    The clock bar returns to the off-duty state after the clock-out is recorded.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ records

LifelineIQ records your clock-out time. If you had an open break, LifelineIQ closes that break when you clock out.

## Common problems

### I do not see Clock Out

**Clock Out** only appears when LifelineIQ has an open clock-in for you. If you believe your time is missing, ask your supervisor to review your time entry.

### I forgot to clock out

Tell your supervisor. A manager can review and correct time entries before payroll.
