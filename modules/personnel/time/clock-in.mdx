Use **Relay \> Today** when you are starting your shift. The clock control appears in the top bar and changes based on whether LifelineIQ already has an open time entry for you.

<Frame caption="Relay Today screen with the Clock In control in the top bar. Personal information is blurred.">
  ![Relay Today page showing the top bar with Clock In, quick actions, schedule information, and work navigation.](/images/screenshots/relay-home.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Older **Connect** links now route users into Relay. Use **Relay** as the current home for field staff workflows.
</Note>

## Before you start

Make sure you are signed in under the correct agency. If you work for more than one agency, check the active agency before recording time.

You need an active LifelineIQ account with permission to use Relay time tracking.

## What this page is for

Clocking in creates the start of your time entry for the day. Managers review time entries later before payroll or timecard export.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Relay">
    Go to **Relay**. The page opens on **Today**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the clock control">
    Look at the top-right area of the Relay header. If you are not clocked in, the control shows **Clock In**.

    On some screen sizes, the same clock control may appear as an icon with the **Clock In** label beside it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check that you are off duty">
    If the page shows **Off Duty** or the button says **Clock In**, LifelineIQ does not have an open time entry for you.

    If the page shows **On Duty** or the action says **Clock Out**, you are already clocked in and should not clock in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Clock In">
    Select **Clock In** once when your shift starts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for confirmation">
    LifelineIQ records the clock-in and updates the duty state. The action changes from **Clock In** to **Clock Out** when your time entry is open.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue your Relay workflow">
    After clocking in, use Relay for shift work such as **Truck Check**, **Report Case**, **Time Off**, **Schedule**, **My Tasks**, **Policies**, **Training**, or **HR**, depending on what your agency has enabled.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ records

LifelineIQ records the clock-in time, the active user, agency context, and a pending time entry for review. The entry is not the final payroll record until the timecard review process is completed.

## What good looks like

After clocking in, the Relay header no longer asks you to clock in. It shows the active duty state and gives you the option to **Clock Out** when the shift ends.

## Common problems

### I do not see Clock In

You may already be clocked in. Look for **On Duty** or **Clock Out** in the same top bar area.

### I clicked Clock In but I am not sure it worked

Refresh Relay and look at the clock control again. If it now shows **Clock Out**, your clock-in was recorded.

### I clocked in under the wrong agency

Tell your supervisor or timecard reviewer. Do not create duplicate time entries to fix it yourself unless your agency has told you to do that.

### I forgot to clock in

Tell your supervisor or timecard reviewer. They can review the timecard workflow and make the appropriate correction according to agency policy.
