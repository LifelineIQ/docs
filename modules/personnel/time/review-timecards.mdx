Managers review timecards before time is finalized or exported for payroll.

## Before you start

Open **Schedule** and go to **Timecards**. The current LifelineIQ route is `/schedule/timecards`.

You need permission to review personnel timekeeping records.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open timecards">
    Go to **Schedule** and open **Timecards**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the pay period">
    Select the pay period you need to review.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review exceptions">
    Look for missed clock-ins, pending entries, schedule differences, rejected entries, and entries that need correction.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open a person's timecard">
    Review the clock-in, clock-out, break time, scheduled time, and total hours.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approve when ready">
    Approve the timecard only after the time looks correct.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ records

Approved timecards can be included in payroll export. Entries that still have issues should be corrected before export.

## Common problems

### Someone missed a clock-in

Review the timecard detail and correct the time entry before approval.

### A shift does not match the schedule

Use the schedule difference and reconciliation information to decide whether the time should be accepted or adjusted.
