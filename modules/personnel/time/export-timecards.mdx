Export timecards after the pay period has been reviewed and approved.

## Before you start

Review pending and exception timecards first. Exporting is intended for time that is ready for payroll.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open timecards">
    Go to **Schedule** and open **Timecards**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the pay period">
    Choose the period you want to export.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm timecards are approved">
    Check that the people included in the export are approved and ready.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export">
    Select **Export** and choose the export options your payroll process needs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ records

Exported timecards are marked so managers can see which entries were already sent through the payroll workflow.

## Common problems

### A person is missing from the export

Check whether their timecard is approved for the selected period.

### The hours look wrong

Return to timecard review and correct the entry before exporting again.
