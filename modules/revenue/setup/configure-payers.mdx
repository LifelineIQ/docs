Payer setup controls how billing teams identify, configure, and work payer-specific claims.

## Before you start

Open **Revenue** and go to **Payers**. Current routes include `/revenue/finance/payers` and `/admin/revenue/payers`.

You need permission to manage revenue payer settings.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Payers">
    Go to **Revenue** and open **Payers**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find or add the payer">
    Search for the payer first so you do not create a duplicate.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review payer details">
    Check payer identity, billing settings, manuals, scorecard information, and any payer-specific requirements.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure billing behavior">
    Set the payer options your agency needs before claims are worked against that payer.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and test in workflow">
    Confirm billers can use the payer correctly on a claim.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ changes

Payer setup affects claim workflow behavior, billing readiness, payer follow-up, reporting, and finance review.

## Common problems

### A payer already exists

Use the existing payer record instead of creating a duplicate.

### A biller cannot find the payer

Confirm the payer is active and available to the correct agency.
