Use the claims worklist to review claim details, fix issues, and move the claim toward submission.

## Before you start

Open **Revenue** and go to **Claims**. The current route is `/revenue/claims`.

You need access to revenue claims for the active agency.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Claims">
    Go to **Revenue** and open **Claims**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the claim">
    Use the worklist filters, search, or queue status to find the claim you need.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the claim">
    Open the claim record to review patient, trip, coverage, coding, charge, and submission details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the workflow">
    Open the claim workflow when you need to move through billing steps in order.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Resolve issues">
    Fix missing information, coverage problems, coding issues, charge issues, or validation warnings before submission.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Move to the next queue">
    When the claim is ready, move it to the next appropriate status or queue for submission or follow-up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ changes

LifelineIQ keeps the claim record, workflow state, notes, validation state, and billing history together so the next user can see what changed and what still needs work.

## Common problems

### The claim is not ready to submit

Check the workflow sections for missing coverage, coding, charges, signatures, or validation issues.

### I opened the wrong view

Use the claim record for the full claim history. Use the claim workflow when you need guided billing steps.

### The claim is in another queue

Check follow-up, collections, complete, deductible holds, submission queue, and pre-billing queues if the claim does not appear in the main claims list.
