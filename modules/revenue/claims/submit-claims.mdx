Use the submission queue to review claims that are ready to be sent to the payer or clearinghouse.

## Before you start

Open **Revenue** and go to the claim submission queue. The current route is `/revenue/claims/submission-queue`.

Claims should be worked and validated before they reach this queue.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the submission queue">
    Go to **Revenue** and open **Submission Queue**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the claim list">
    Check which claims are ready, which need attention, and which are waiting on another step.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open claims with issues">
    Open any claim that has warnings or missing information before submitting it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit eligible claims">
    Submit only claims that are complete, valid, and ready for payer processing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Watch the response">
    Review submission status and follow-up work after claims are sent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ changes

LifelineIQ records submission activity and keeps the claim's billing lifecycle updated for follow-up, denial review, or completion.

## Common problems

### A claim is missing from the queue

Return to the claim workflow and confirm the claim is ready for submission.

### A claim has validation warnings

Open the claim, resolve the issue, and return to the submission queue.
