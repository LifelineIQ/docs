Use the denials workbench to review payer denials and decide whether to correct, appeal, rebill, or close the issue.

## Before you start

Open **Revenue** and go to **Denials**. The current route is `/revenue/denials`.

You need access to revenue denial workflows.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Denials">
    Go to **Revenue** and open **Denials**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a denial to review">
    Use filters or queues to focus on the denials you are responsible for.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review payer information">
    Check the denial reason, payer response, claim details, coverage, coding, and submission history.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the next action">
    Decide whether the claim needs correction, appeal, rebill, follow-up, or closure.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Document the work">
    Add notes or complete the required workflow steps so the next user knows what happened.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ changes

LifelineIQ keeps denial work tied to the claim record so future billing actions and reporting reflect the denial outcome.

## Common problems

### The denial reason is unclear

Review the payer response and submission history, then compare it with the claim workflow details.

### The claim needs correction before rebilling

Return to the claim workflow, make the correction, and send it through the appropriate next queue.
