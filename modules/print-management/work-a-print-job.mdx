Use **Subscription Print Queue** to manage subscription print jobs.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the queue">
    Go to **Print Management > Subscription Print Queue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the job">
    Select the print job you need to work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review job details">
    Review the job status, recipient or batch information, and print batches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark ready">
    Select **Mark Ready** when the job is ready to print.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start printing">
    Select **Start Printing** when printing begins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the job">
    Select **Mark Completed** after printing is finished.
  </Step>
</Steps>
