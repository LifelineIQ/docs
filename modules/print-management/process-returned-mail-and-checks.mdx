Use Print Management incoming workflows for returned mail and check payments.

## Scan returned mail

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Subscription Intake">
    Go to **Print Management > Subscription Intake**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Scan & process">
    Use the **Scan & process** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan returned mailers">
    Scan barcodes from returned mailers and follow the intake wizard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review history">
    Use intake history to confirm what was processed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enter a check payment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Check Payments">
    Go to **Print Management > Check Payments**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the claim">
    Search by claim number or patient name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the claim">
    Choose the claim row.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payment">
    Complete the check payment form and save the payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>
