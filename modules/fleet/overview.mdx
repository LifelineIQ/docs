Fleet is where operations and maintenance teams keep vehicles ready for service.

Use Fleet when you need to check unit readiness, review inspection history, report a maintenance issue, manage work orders, track preventive maintenance, or review fuel activity.

## Where to go

- Open **Fleet** for the fleet dashboard.
- Open **Vehicles** to find a unit or vehicle record.
- Open **Inspections** to review truck check and DVIR history.
- Open **Work Orders** to manage vehicle and building work.
- Open **PM Schedule** for preventive maintenance.
- Open **Fuel** for fuel activity.
- Open **Admin > Fleet** for units, PM templates, inspection templates, alerts, integrations, categories, and vendors.

<Note>
  Older Maintenance links still open the current Fleet pages. Use Fleet in new training and saved links.
</Note>
