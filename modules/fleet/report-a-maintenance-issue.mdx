Use **Report Maintenance Issue** when you find a vehicle problem that maintenance needs to review.

## Before you start

Have the unit, issue type, and enough detail for maintenance to find the problem. Add photos when they help.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Fleet">
    Go to **Fleet**, then open **Work Orders**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the report">
    Select **Report Issue** or **Report Maintenance Issue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the unit">
    Select the affected unit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the issue">
    Add the details needed to find or reproduce the problem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Classify the issue">
    Choose the issue category, component, severity, and priority.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add photos if needed">
    Attach photos when they make the problem easier to understand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the report">
    Select **Report Issue**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

LifelineIQ creates a work order for maintenance review. A maintenance user can open the work order, update status, add labor or parts, attach files, and close it when the issue is resolved.

## Common problems

### I do not see the unit

Check that you are in the correct agency and that the unit exists in Fleet or Admin Fleet settings.

### I am not sure how urgent it is

Choose the safest priority. Maintenance can adjust the priority after review.

### The issue came from a failed inspection

Open **Fleet > Inspections** and use the work-order action on the failed or conditional inspection when available.
