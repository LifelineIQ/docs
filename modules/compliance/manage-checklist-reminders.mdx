Use **Compliance settings** to control the in-app and email reminders LifelineIQ sends for agency compliance checklists. Reminders are off by default so your agency chooses what staff receive.

## Prerequisites

- Access to **Admin > Compliance** or **Compliance > Settings** (admin role).
- At least one active agency checklist with a due date. Add checklists under **Your checklists** on the same page.

## Reminder types

Each toggle is independent. When a toggle is on, LifelineIQ sends the reminder in-app and by email to the assignees for that cycle.

| Reminder | When it sends | Who receives it |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Expiration reminders** | The configured number of days before the checklist's due date. | Assignees who have not completed the current cycle. |
| **Overdue alerts** | After the due date passes. | Assignees who have not completed the current cycle. |
| **New content** | When a new checklist is assigned. | Every staff member the checklist targets. |

For per-person checklists, each staff member gets their own reminder. For whole-agency checklists, the reminder goes to the agency inbox for the cycle.

## Turn on reminders

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open compliance settings">
    Go to **Compliance > Settings** and open the **Notifications** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the reminder types you want">
    Turn on **Expiration reminders**, **Overdue alerts**, or **New content**. You can enable any combination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the expiration lead time">
    With **Expiration reminders** on, enter how many days before the due date the reminder should go out. Valid range is 1 to 90 days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and verify">
    Changes save as you toggle. Check **Completion** to confirm assignees appear for the current cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Track who still needs to finish

Open the **Completion** section on the same page for the current cycle of each active checklist.

- Cycles past their due date show an **Overdue** badge.
- Per-person checklists include a **Who's left** drill-down that lists the staff who have not completed the current cycle.
- The completion column shows how many eligible staff finished, with a running percentage.

Use the drill-down to follow up directly with staff instead of resending a blanket reminder.

## Common problems

### Staff say they did not get a reminder

Confirm the matching toggle is on in **Notifications**, and that the staff member is an active assignee for the current cycle. Reminders never re-send for the same cycle, so if a toggle was off when the window passed, that cycle will not backfill.

### Expiration reminders send too early or too late

Adjust **Days before due to send expiration reminders**. Increase the value for longer lead time, or lower it for a closer-to-due nudge.

### A checklist is not on the completion list

Only active checklists with a due date appear. Open **Your checklists** and confirm the checklist is active and has a first due date.
