## Where to go

- Open **Compliance** for the dashboard.
- Open **Documents** for the document repository.
- Open **Program** for compliance program work and exports.
- Open **Agency Authority** for agency authority tracking.
- Open **Goals** for compliance metrics and targets.
- Open **Admin > Compliance** for compliance settings and resources.

<Note>
  Compliance incident links open Safety incidents. Use Safety for incident review and investigation work.
</Note>
