Use a payment link when the agency sends a secure link for a bill or balance.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the payment link">
    Open the link provided by the agency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the payment page">
    Confirm the page is for the expected agency and payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Enter payment details and submit payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm completion">
    Wait for the **Payment Complete** confirmation before leaving the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return if needed">
    Use **Return to Patient Portal** or **Go to Patient Portal** when available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### The link is invalid

Request a new payment link from the agency billing department.

### Payment was not confirmed

Try again from the payment page. If it still fails, contact the agency.
