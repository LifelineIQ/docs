Use **Supplies** for consumable inventory and **Equipment** for devices, gear, and durable equipment.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Inventory">
    Go to **Inventory**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the item type">
    Open **Supplies** for medical supplies, medications, or consumables. Open **Equipment** for devices and gear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the item">
    Select **Add Supply** or **Add Equipment**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter item details">
    Add the name, SKU or identifier, stock details, location details, reorder information, and any condition or tracking fields that apply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the item">
    Save the item so it appears in the Supplies or Equipment list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Other ways to add items

- Use **Field Add** when a user needs to add inventory from the field workflow.
- Use import actions when you need to load many items at once.
- Use hospital restock or movement actions when the work is about stock movement instead of a new catalog item.

## Common problems

### I cannot find the item after saving

Clear search filters and check whether it was added under Supplies or Equipment.

### I need to move stock between locations

Use **Pick Lists** or inventory movement tools instead of creating a duplicate item.
