## Where to go

- Open **Inventory** for the inventory overview.
- Open **Supplies** for medical supplies, medications, and consumables.
- Open **Expiring Supplies** to review supply lots that are expired or expiring within 30 days.
- Open **Equipment** for devices, gear, and equipment condition.
- Open **Orders** for purchase orders.
- Open **Pick Lists** for transfers, restocks, and kit refills.
- Open **Audits** for stock counts and discrepancy review.
- Open **Locations** for stock locations.
- Open **Field** for field inventory work.
- Open **Admin > Inventory** for vendor setup.

<Note>
  Inventory reports now open through Intelligence reports.
</Note>
