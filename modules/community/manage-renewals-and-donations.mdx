Use Community marketing pages to manage renewal and donation outreach.

## Manage renewals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Renewals">
    Go to **Community > Marketing > Renewals**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review renewal work">
    Review the renewal letters and any renewal worklist items shown on the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare or update letters">
    Use the renewal letter tools to prepare the next outreach step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm follow-up">
    Make sure the renewal status or next action reflects what should happen next.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage donations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Donations">
    Go to **Community > Donations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review donation work">
    Check donation activity and outreach worklist items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work event donations">
    Use the Event Donations tab when the donation is tied to a community event.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update follow-up">
    Record the next outreach or follow-up step based on your agency process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### I need to change default renewal or donation settings

Use **Admin > Community** and open the renewal or donation settings area.

### I expected this to be under Events

Marketing and renewals moved out of the Events sub-area. Use **Community > Marketing** or **Community > Marketing > Renewals**.
