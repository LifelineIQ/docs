## Where to go

- Open **Community** for the hub.
- Open **Events** for the event calendar and event records.
- Open **Partners** for partner work.
- Open **Facility Events** or **Facility Visit Reminders** for facility outreach.
- Open **Grants** for the grant pipeline.
- Open **Donations** for donation work.
- Open **Marketing** for subscription marketing plans.
- Open **Renewals** for renewal letters.
- Open **Subscriptions** for campaign and intake work.
- Open **Admin > Community** for event, donation, grant, marketing, and renewal settings.

<Note>
  Medical alerts are documented under Patients because medical alert records are patient-based.
</Note>
