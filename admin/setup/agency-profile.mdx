Use **Admin > Agency Setup** for the agency identity and documents LifelineIQ uses across modules.

## Agency setup sections

| Section | What it controls | Downstream effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Organization** | Legal identity, agency details, address, licenses, and identifiers. | Billing, records, reporting, public forms, and operational context. |
| **Contacts** | Billing, operations, compliance, and agency contact people. | Internal follow-up, onboarding, escalations, and support. |
| **Branding** | Logos, colors, and staff-facing app appearance. | LifelineIQ visual identity and public-facing surfaces where enabled. |
| **Documents** | Agency policy documents and setup files. | Compliance, onboarding, and administrative reference. |

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Agency Setup">
    Go to **Admin > Agency Setup > Agency Setup**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Organization">
    Enter the agency identity and required organization details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Contacts">
    Add the correct billing, operations, compliance, and administrative contacts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Branding">
    Upload approved branding and review the visual settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Documents">
    Add the documents the agency wants to keep available for setup and compliance reference.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify affected workflows">
    Check public forms, billing setup, onboarding, and operational modules that use agency identity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common problems

### The wrong agency is showing

Check the agency selector before saving. Admin changes apply to the selected agency context.

### A document contains sensitive information

Do not upload documents with PHI unless the document belongs in a protected LifelineIQ workflow and your agency policy allows it.
