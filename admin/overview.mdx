Admin is where authorized users configure LifelineIQ.

The Admin sidebar is organized by the job the setting supports:

- **Agency Setup**: organization, contacts, branding, documents, and security.
- **Lifeline Respond**: Home, Dispatch, Chart, MIH, and Fire settings.
- **Lifeline Quality**: Quality, Safety, and Compliance settings.
- **Lifeline People**: Provider, Education, Patients, and Community settings.
- **Lifeline Revenue**: claims, communications, finance, payer setup, facilities, enrollment, and EDI.
- **Lifeline Logistics**: Fleet, Inventory, and IT settings.
- **Lifeline Insight**: data, integrations, automation, policies, workflows, and AI agents.

## How to use Admin

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Admin">
    Select **Admin** in LifelineIQ.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the job area">
    Open the category that matches the work, such as **Lifeline Revenue** or **Lifeline People**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the module">
    Open the module group, such as **Revenue**, **Provider**, **Dispatch**, or **Chart**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the setting">
    Select the setting page you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and verify downstream">
    Save changes, then check the operational workflow affected by the setting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Admin changes can affect production workflows. Use synthetic examples in docs and training, and never paste PHI or real patient data into setup notes.
</Warning>
