Use **Admin > Lifeline People > Provider > Schedule** settings to control how scheduling works.

## Schedule settings

| Setting | What it controls |
| --- | --- |
| **Schedule Builder** | Core schedule builder defaults. |
| **Schedule Rules** | Rules that guide schedule creation and validation. |
| **Payroll & Overtime** | Payroll and overtime behavior. |
| **Comp Time** | Comp time behavior. |
| **Notifications** | Schedule-related notifications. |
| **Leave Types** | Leave categories used in requests and balances. |
| **PTO Policies** | PTO behavior and policy rules. |
| **Team Schedule Visibility** | What staff can see in team schedule views. |
| **Availability** | Availability settings used by schedule workflows. |

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open schedule settings">
    Go to **Admin > Lifeline People > Provider**, then open the schedule settings section you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure builder defaults">
    Start with **Schedule Builder** before changing rules or downstream workflows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure rules and payroll">
    Set **Schedule Rules**, **Payroll & Overtime**, and **Comp Time** based on agency policy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure leave and PTO">
    Set **Leave Types** and **PTO Policies** before staff submit requests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure visibility and availability">
    Set **Team Schedule Visibility** and **Availability** so staff see and submit the right information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify in Scheduling">
    Open Scheduling and test build, request, and timecard workflows after changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
