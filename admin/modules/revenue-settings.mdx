Use **Admin > Lifeline Revenue > Revenue** for billing and revenue configuration.

## Revenue settings groups

| Group | Settings |
| --- | --- |
| **Claims** | Claim Rules, Queue Management, Insurance Defaults, Cancel & Void, Claim Tags, Status Reasons, Quick Replies, Patient Billing. |
| **Communications** | Texting, Calls, Activity, IVR Results, Lifecycle Campaigns, Surveys, Message Templates. |
| **Finance** | Rates & Service Levels, Fee Schedule tools, Contract Grades, Payer Groups, Collections, Collection Agencies, Deductible Monitoring, Profit Centers, Accounting Periods, Accounting Export, Benchmarks. |
| **Payers and facilities** | Payers and Facilities. |
| **Enrollment** | Agency Licensing, Banking & Treasury, Payer Enrollment, Payer Portal Logins, Waystar, AI Follow-Up Agent. |
| **EDI** | EDI & Clearinghouse. |

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Revenue settings">
    Go to **Admin > Lifeline Revenue > Revenue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure claims first">
    Set claim rules, queues, insurance defaults, tags, status reasons, and patient billing behavior.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure payers and facilities">
    Add or update payer and facility settings before claims are submitted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure finance">
    Set rates, fee schedules, collections, profit centers, accounting periods, and exports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure communications">
    Set messaging, campaigns, surveys, templates, IVR, and billing communication defaults.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure enrollment and EDI">
    Confirm agency licensing, banking, payer enrollment, clearinghouse, and Waystar setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify in Revenue">
    Test a non-PHI sample workflow in claims, submission queue, denials, and finance views.
  </Step>
</Steps>
