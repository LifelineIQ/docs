Use **Admin > Lifeline People > Provider** for personnel, lifecycle, compliance, case, and schedule-related setup.

## Provider settings groups

| Group | Settings |
| --- | --- |
| **People standards** | Compliance Standards, Alerts, Probation Period, Cases, Imports and Exports. |
| **People lifecycle** | Onboarding and Updates, Offboarding, HR Approvals, SOG Approvals. |
| **Reference catalogs** | Personnel types and provider data standards. |
| **Scheduling** | Schedule Builder, Schedule Rules, Payroll & Overtime, Comp Time, Notifications, Leave Types, PTO Policies, Team Schedule Visibility, Availability, Coverage Rules. |

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Provider settings">
    Go to **Admin > Lifeline People > Provider**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure people standards">
    Set compliance standards, alerts, probation periods, case settings, and import/export behavior.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure lifecycle workflows">
    Set onboarding, offboarding, HR approvals, and SOG approvals before using personnel lifecycle workflows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure reference catalogs">
    Review personnel types and data standards used by staff records and reporting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure schedule-related settings">
    Review schedule settings that affect staffing, timecards, leave, PTO, and availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify downstream workflows">
    Test personnel records, onboarding, offboarding, approvals, schedule, and timecards after changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
