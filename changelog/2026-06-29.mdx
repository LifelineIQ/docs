A big week for Revenue and Dispatch, plus a refreshed look across Admin, Home, and mobile.

## New features

### Denials worklist and analytics
Denied claims now sort themselves into action lanes so billers see what to work next, and a new denial analytics dashboard shows trends over time. Each denial opens with the full CARC/RARC explanation, the original 835/277, and any prior denial history in one view. See [Work denials](/modules/revenue/claims/work-denials).

### Call payer from a claim
A **Call payer** button on the claim Overview tab places a voice call to the payer, runs a denial-specific script, and logs the outcome back to the claim. Useful for fast follow-up on aged or denied claims.

### Corrected-claim and COB resubmits
You can now resubmit corrected claims for modifier and demographic fixes directly from the claim, and switch the primary payer on a coordination-of-benefits claim without a manual resend. The correction dialog shows exactly which COB payer is being billed.

### Pre-bill validation gates
Claims now run eligibility, term-date, other-insured DOB, and ambulance-modifier checks before submission. Claims with a date of service after coverage terminates are blocked, and MCO-primary issues are flagged. Inpatient (POS 21) claims require an admission date.

### Secondary (Medicaid) billing
Secondary Medicaid billing has been repaired and wired up end-to-end with auto-submit support.

### Payer portal playbooks and MFA hand-off
Read-only NaviNet and Availity portal playbooks now run inside LifelineIQ, with a real-time human MFA hand-off when a payer challenges the session. An eligibility run type lets the agent fetch coverage straight from the portal.

### Destination decision support
Dispatch now recommends transport destinations based on facility capabilities and current diversion status. See the destination card on the incident view.

### Geofence-driven status updates
911 incidents can auto-advance to **En route** and **On scene** when a unit crosses configured geofences. Set up zones in **Admin → Dispatch → Geofencing**.

### Station displays
Station displays got several new widgets: weather radar with all-alerts severity, a celebrations widget for birthdays and work anniversaries, dispatch-takeover audio, and a response timer with route-to-scene during active incidents. Displays can also run in portrait orientation. See [Create a station display](/modules/display/create-a-station-display).

### Active911-parity alert detail
The mobile and web alert detail screens were redesigned for Active911 parity, with a new **Arrive at station** status.

### Fire reporting on real data
Fire Activities/CRR, Scheduling & Staffing, Personnel, Properties & Preplans, and Assets & Logistics are now wired to live data instead of placeholders. NERIS submissions now reconcile async accept/reject responses and support resubmit after rejection. See [Submit to NERIS](/modules/fire-reporting/submit-to-neris).

### Inventory: lots, batches, and expiration
Supplies are now tracked by lot batch and expiration date, and expirations show when staff take product out of stock. Inventory status reflects total stock across all locations.

### MIH consent and education tracking
MIH now sends consent follow-up and renewal reminders automatically and tracks whether enrolled patients have read the education you send them, tied to pathway goals.

### Configurable agency rank
Agencies can now configure their own personnel **Rank** field from Admin.

### Supervisor fatigue overrides
Supervisors can record fatigue overrides and waivers when assigning shifts that would otherwise be blocked.

### Scheduled report subscriptions by role
Scheduled reports can now target roles and groups, not just individual users.

## Updates

### Admin and navigation refresh
Admin pages now share a single side-rail layout with a completion ring for required fields. The agency profile setup meter is a clickable "% complete" menu. Module switcher groups are organized into branded Lifeline sections. The Fleet module has been renamed **Maintenance** in navigation.

### Revenue navigation
The Revenue dashboard has been consolidated, with claims-by-stage counts on the sidebar. Payment Posting is now a hub with a switcher for Refunds, Underpayments, and Bank Rec. Contracts moved from a standalone page into the per-payer Payor view and a facility Contracts tab.

### Claim rules engine
Claim rules now support:
- Conditions on secondary and tertiary payers.
- A positional service-line modifier action.
- A **stop-after-match** flag for rule exceptions.
- A documentation attach + reprocess action.

### CAD board and dispatch map
- Edit a unit's live activation (crew and hours) directly from the CAD board.
- Right-click the dispatch map to start an incident at that location.
- Select and dispatch units from the map.
- Pop the CAD map out into its own window.
- CAD board lanes now gate to staffed shifts or assigned work.
- Dispatch uses the standard collapsible nav.

See [Use the CAD board](/modules/dispatch/use-the-cad-board).

### Scheduling
- Month view shows every shift with per-day tiles and snaps to today.
- Rotation order is honored within every schedule group.
- Schedule coverage gaps are readable and clickable.
- A traditional-calendar toggle is available on the schedule toolbar.
- A leave-type reason drop-down appears when marking someone out.
- Personnel and scheduling users can load `/schedule` without the CAD module.

See [Build a schedule](/modules/personnel/schedule/build-a-schedule).

### Timecards and timekeeping
PTO and leave now appear on timecard reconciliation. Timecard exports report the real pay-period window. Agency admins can save pay-period and FLSA config. See [Review timecards](/modules/personnel/time/review-timecards).

### Home dashboard
- The Home calendar is clickable and deep-links into the schedule.
- The performance metrics widget is configurable, with crew-based attribution.
- Pin specific KB articles or policies as Home resources.
- Slack-style multi-emoji reactions on the home feed.
- Suggested resources and empty-widget hints in the dashboard builder.

### Chart editor
The chart editor navigation has been condensed for faster field charting, and the status stack now collapses into the header. No-patient, cancelled, MCI, and attach/CAD actions are surfaced inline.

### CRM and onboarding
Agency onboarding is now integrated directly into the CRM account tab. CRM accounts gained firmographics, lead source, branding, and relationships; segmentation by region and segment with filters; and an editable RFP Overview. Account health is now derived from real signals.

### Mobile redesign
A multi-week mobile redesign continues — softer cards, lighter headings, a clean large-title shell, a Today widget board, and refreshed Clock, Messages, Alerts, Connect, and Directory screens.

### Brand and login
The login page got a cinematic hero refresh with an EKG motif and a reframed session-expired flow. Brand green is now the action color across the app, with the lime hue retired.
