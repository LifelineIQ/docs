Most LifelineIQ workflows start from the module in the left navigation. The pages in this site use the same module names you see in the app.

## Common starting points

- **Relay**: Start a shift, view your schedule, handle tasks, and open everyday team tools.
- **Schedule**: Build shifts, assign people, publish drafts, and review schedule requests.
- **Personnel**: Manage employee records, onboarding, offboarding, credentials, and compliance.
- **Timecards**: Review clock-ins, corrections, approvals, and payroll exports.

## If you work in more than one agency

Check the active agency before you make a change. Clock-ins, schedules, personnel records, and timecards are agency-scoped.
