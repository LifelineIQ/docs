## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open LifelineIQ">
    Sign in to LifelineIQ using your approved account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to time tracking">
    Open the time tracking area from the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your work location">
    Confirm that the displayed location or work context is correct before clocking in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clock in">
    Select **Clock in** and wait for the confirmation message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your status">
    Make sure your status shows that you are clocked in before starting work.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

After you clock in, LifelineIQ records the start time for your shift. Your status updates so authorized team members can see that you are available for work.

## Common problems

### I do not see the time tracking area

Your role may not include time tracking access. Ask an administrator to confirm your permissions.

### The clock-in button is unavailable

You may already be clocked in, outside the expected time window, or missing required setup. Review the message on the page and contact your supervisor if it does not resolve the issue.

### My location or work context is wrong

Do not clock in until the issue is corrected. Contact an administrator so they can review your profile or site assignment.
