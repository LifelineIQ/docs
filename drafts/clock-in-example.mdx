Use the clock bar on the Relay home page when you start and end your shift.

<Note>
  Open **Relay** from LifelineIQ. If you use an older **Connect** link, LifelineIQ sends you to Relay.
</Note>

## Before you start

Make sure you are signed in to the correct agency. If you work for more than one agency, check the agency shown in LifelineIQ before you clock in.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Relay">
    Go to **Relay**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the clock bar">
    Look near the top of the Relay home page for the clock bar.

    If you are not clocked in, it shows **Off Duty** and a **Clock In** button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clock in">
    Select **Clock In**.

    LifelineIQ confirms the clock-in with this message: **Clocked in**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm you are on duty">
    The clock bar changes to **On Duty** and starts showing your shift time.

    If you see **On Duty**, LifelineIQ already has an open clock-in for you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clock out at the end of your shift">
    When your shift is over, return to Relay and select **Clock Out**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What LifelineIQ records

When you clock in, LifelineIQ records your clock-in time and marks the entry as pending for review.

When you clock out, LifelineIQ records your clock-out time. If you had an open break, LifelineIQ closes that break at the same time.

## Common problems

### I do not see Clock In

If the clock bar shows **On Duty**, you are already clocked in. Use **Clock Out** when your shift ends.

### I clicked Clock In but it did not work

Refresh Relay and check the clock bar again. If it now shows **On Duty**, your clock-in was recorded.

If it still shows **Off Duty**, contact your supervisor or LifelineIQ administrator.

### I do not see Clock Out

**Clock Out** only appears when LifelineIQ has an open clock-in for you. If you believe you are missing a clock-in, ask your supervisor to review your time entry.

## Manager follow-up

Clock-ins are not final payroll records by themselves. A manager still reviews pending time entries in the personnel timekeeping workflow before they are finalized.
